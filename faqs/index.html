Frequently Asked Questions

Got a question? We’ve got an answer!

Is it really free? What's the catch?

Catholify is free in every sense of the word—there is NO catch! We will never sell the app nor any of your personal data to advertisers, as we feel this would sacrifice the heart and purpose of our mission. We are able to do this only by gracious givers like you who make it all possible.

Can I request a feature?

The whole purpose and mission of Catholify is to build for the Church what she needs. WE are the Church! We very much want to hear what you have to say, so please reach out to us at team@catholify.com.

My app is crashing – how do I fix it?

We're sorry you're having trouble! Please email us at support@catholify.com detailing as much as you can about the situation (what type of device you are on, what steps to take to reproduce the problem, etc.) and we'll get back with you as soon as we can.

I have a question about something else

We'd be happy to answer it! Please direct all general inquiries to help@catholify.com.

What if One Million Rosaries Were Prayed for One Million Souls?

Last year, all of us came together on a mission to pray and log 1 Million Rosaries—and we did it! This year, the challenge is greater, but so is the reward. Whenever you pray a Rosary, come back here and tap “I Prayed A Rosary“ to claim and send your personalized prayer card to someone! Join us in spreading this movement across social media to #PrayItForward so that 1 million souls may be personally touched by your prayers!
