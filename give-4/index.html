Support Catholic creators and their fruits 🍇

To build a better platform, we must adopt a different model: that good content is worth supporting. Through recurring subscriptions, we can build an ethical business model that doesn’t involve tracking or selling your data.

We are committed to building a better home for Catholic creators to be discovered and supported by you, so they can bear more fruit for their families and for the culture who desperately needs them.

How to get involved

Pray

If we didn’t believe in the power of prayer, we wouldn’t be here. If you want to take ownership of Catholify with us, it starts by praying for this mission. Beyond that, commit to using the prayer tools Catholify has already made available, and invite others to do the same.

Serve

The best way you can serve is by using and sharing the app, connecting with us on social media, and making your voice known! Have a feature you want to see added? Are you a blog writer? Marketing guru? Graphic designer? We want to become a hub of talent for the Church’s digital presence, but that takes a much wider net than just us!

Give

Join the mission in a big way by making us a part of your monthly tithe so that we can continue to pioneer new platforms of prayer for the Church!

What if One Million Rosaries Were Prayed for One Million Souls?

Last year, all of us came together on a mission to pray and log 1 Million Rosaries—and we did it! This year, the challenge is greater, but so is the reward. Whenever you pray a Rosary, come back here and tap “I Prayed A Rosary“ to claim and send your personalized prayer card to someone! Join us in spreading this movement across social media to #PrayItForward so that 1 million souls may be personally touched by your prayers!
