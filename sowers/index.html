Finally: A platform for Catholic

Introducing the Sowers’ Circle: the growth network for Catholic businesses and influencers that outperforms social media

The problem isn't the content,

As a Catholic professional, you carry the message of Goodness and Truth throughout your work. But spreading that message and growing a supportive audience is becoming increasingly difficult online. You need a platform that wants to grow your mission as much as you do, for the livelihood of your family and the good of the Church. The Sowers’ Circle accomplishes this in two primary ways:

The Harvest

The Harvest is our daily curated feed to promote our Sowers' content, goods and services—free from the noise, toxicity and censorship of social media.

The Circle

The Circle is our professionals network for Sowers to connect and collaborate. It also serves as the gateway for submitting your works to The Harvest.

Tell me more

It begins with
The Harvest

Say goodbye to the feed, say hello to The Harvest…

Only Your Content

Social media promises everyone a “shot at fame”…if you’re loud enough. This model breeds ego, sensation and content overload. We believe only the finest content, goods and services should be showcased—yours.

Refreshingly Relevant

Instead of infinite feeds that sell your data to the highest bidder, The Harvest hand-curates a liturgically-relevant daily roundup of joy, discovery and formation driven by your business, your story, and the fruits of your labor.

Widespread Reach
The Harvest is designed to cast the widest net possible—through our newsletter, website, mobile app, and social media. We even have opportunities to be featured on our YouTube channel.
Your Audience Is Yours
Social media networks can take away your hard earned audience at a moment’s notice. It’s time for that to change.

A better platform deserves
a better audience

Better access to better fruits like yours is only the first step. The culture has reached digital burnout and needs a more balanced alternative when it comes to content and commerce.

With Catholify, we are conditioning consumers to be better stewards by encouraging and rewarding patronage to their favorite creators—you! As we do, we’re building better tools for you to connect and monetize with this increasingly motivated audience.

Become a Sower

Putting the power back in your hands

With social media, you own your content but they own your audience (which they can take away at will). Yet, email has consistently proven to be 40x more effective and remains one of the most dependable and censorship-free mediums available. That’s why we believe you should share in the bounty of The Harvest and grow your own mailing list in the process:

Here's Your Hook

Need something to offer your audience that's worthy of them sharing their email? Give the gift of The Harvest with your personalized signup page that joins them to your mailing list in the process! (See a sample)

Your Efforts Rewarded

We think it's only fair that the more subscribers you bring on, the more opportunities you'll earn for placement in The Harvest—regardless of the membership tier you're in!

Benefit Together

When we mutually collaborate, we mutually benefit. All sowers working to cultivate The Harvest yields that much larger of an engaged audience for you to reach in the future.

Why Bother?

Simply put, the Church needs a safe haven for information sharing. A place where values are cultivated, your works are elevated, and widespread calls-to-action are possible if and when the need arises. We all need The Harvest, and we need your help to grow it!

The Circle: A network of
like-minded leaders

A community of professionals

What the Harvest does for external growth, the Circle does for internal growth! Surround yourself with fellow Catholic professionals, freelancers, artists, makers, laborers, and ministry leaders. No need to make connections first. If you’re here, you’re already family!

Built-in marketing opportunities
We built the Sowers’ Circle not just as a professional network, but as a launchpad for promoting your works and accomplishments—AKA Fruits!—for community feedback and placement on The Harvest. Whatever your promotional needs are, we can help you achieve them!
An incentivized support network
A mission-aligned community doesn’t just post its own fruits, but reacts-to and supports the works of others! Our curation staff might even highlight you and your comment of someone else’s work in The Harvest for bonus exposure ✨
Refreshingly and fully Catholic

Each member of the Sowers’ Circle is held to a standard of living out and upholding the full teachings of the Magisterium of the Catholic Church and doing so with charity, dignity and joy through their professional lives. In a culture of sensation and eroding values, the world needs leaders like you standing lovingly and uncompromisingly upon the firm foundation of Truth. Share and cultivate these values with us in the Sowers’ Circle.

Plans that scale you

We are here to cultivate the fruits of those who share our mission and are ready to take part in it. Join us in building a better network that elevates the culture through the True, Good and Beautiful works you bring to the world!
