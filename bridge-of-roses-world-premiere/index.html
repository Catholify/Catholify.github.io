A Call To Confraternity

The entire purpose of this film is to promote the renewal and reinvigoration of the Confraternity of the Holy Rosary. The graces associated with praying the Rosary as a member of the Confraternity are exponentially more powerful—to the extent that it is worth making a documentary about it! 

It is the hope of the Marian Devotional Movement and Catholify that you would take this simple yet powerful step to enroll in the confraternity through any of the worldwide shrines below:

Our Lady of the Cape – Canada
Our Lady of Good Help – USA
Shrine of the Immaculate Heart – USA
The Rosary Shrine – UK

“If anyone has the happiness of being in the Confraternity of the Rosary, he has in all corners of the world brothers and sisters who pray for him.”

– St. John Vianney

Join us in spreading this movement across social media to #PrayItForward so that 1 million souls may be personally touched by your prayers!
