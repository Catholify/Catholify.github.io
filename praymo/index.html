Looking for live prayer? We are eternally grateful for the hundreds of prayer leaders who committed to praying live regularly, especially during the pandemic. Our Praymo calendar served as the "TV Guide for live prayer" and was an incredibly valuable resource for those looking for prayer community. As we enter back into a post-pandemic world, many leaders have adopted a less regular live prayer regiment. As a result, we have decided to take the calendar down and let you continue to follow those leaders you were most fond of on their personal channels. If you are looking for regular community and leader content, we encourage you to join us in the Catholify app for the daily feeds and comments.

What if One Million Rosaries Were Prayed for One Million Souls?

Last year, all of us came together on a mission to pray and log 1 Million Rosaries—and we did it! This year, the challenge is greater, but so is the reward. Whenever you pray a Rosary, come back here and tap “I Prayed A Rosary“ to claim and send your personalized prayer card to someone! Join us in spreading this movement across social media to #PrayItForward so that 1 million souls may be personally touched by your prayers!
